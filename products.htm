Children are given a dollar and watch it dwindle as lights are left on, food is wasted, water bills rise, possessions are not taken care of, etc. 20 cents is placed in the money pit each time money is wasted. A very graphic teaching tool about how waste affects family finances. Comes with 8 pawns for large LDS families. Order Form
