800 Signs of the Times Booklet

This booklet lists 800 signs of the times in order. The order was achieved by merging 75 chapters from the LDS standard works and trying to list the events in the order each prophet mentions them. A real eye opener as to the magnitude of the information we have been given in relation to future events. Gives a somewhat objective overview of the order of the signs that will happen in these last days.
