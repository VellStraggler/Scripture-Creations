Hosting a Party

Hosting a party should be easy and fun. It can all be done in seven easy steps:
  • Help the Host print, fill out, and send invitations (they may need to send out 30 to 40 invitations to get 8-12 guests).
  • Have your host call the guests the day before to remind them, get RSVPs, and ask them to invite a friend.
  • Print off the order forms, catalogues, information cards, and free gifts
  • Have the party
    • Setup - get there 15 - 30 minutes early to setup and make sure everything is in order.
    • Welcome guests
    • Fill out information cards
    • Game or get to know you activity (preferably using information cards)
    • Explain products
    • Take orders
  • Fax, Mail, or E-Mail in the orders and payment
  • Follow up on Information Cards.
    • Schedule new parties
    • Train new sales reps
    • Online, enter customer data for new product and specials notifications.
  • Receive and distribute the products (Usually just take product to party host and let them distribute)
The party should take only about one hour!
